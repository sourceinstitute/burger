Burger Night

Burger Night is a place for tech founders to hang out. No talks, no investors, no business cards. Just us, catching up, keeping it real.

Burger Night happens every month, at a nice burger joint, usually a cheap one. (Burger Night is boostrapping-friendly.)

If there is a Burger Night in your city, you have to ask around to find it. Some tech founders will know. Right now, there's a Burger Night in Rotterdam and Sofia. (It started in London, then, well, wtf London?)

If there's no Burger Night in your city, you can start one if you're a founder and you follow the rules below. Just let us know so we let everyone know it exists here.

Burger Night isn't advertised anywhere. It's not listed on any meetup site. It has no email list or LinkedIn group. It never has a sponsor. (We buy our own dinner, thank you.) It's self-organised so it can't be owned or branded by a PR or events company. We keep it under the radar of all the hype, consultants, politicians and other crap that gets in the way of running a tech business. It's our space.

We keep it that way by keeping it to founders only. We welcome all kinds of tech founders, but let's keep it to founders only please.
