Fixer Sessions

Fixer Sessions allow a single Challenger to have a particular challenge solved by a small group of peers (Fixers). In doing so, the Challenger also learns how the Fixers approach the challenge, so learns to broaden their own mindset. The Challenger also learns how to delegate to leaders and highly-responsible people who respond better with "Solve for X" objectives than task-based micro-management.

We're running Fixers on AI-based challenges every Friday at Betahaus Sofia. Attendance is limited to 5 people per session.

Each session is facilitated to a strict structure, which starts with guided delegation after which the Fixers ask clarifi cation questions about the objective and deliverables. Then the Fixers work with each other while the Challenger remains a silent observer. The session ends with a presentation by the Fixers and feedback from the Challenger.
