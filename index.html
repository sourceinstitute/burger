Fixer Sessions

Bring a tangible challenge to group of peers to learn from them. Learn their approach and their mindset, and how to delegate.

We're running Fixers on AI-based challenges every Friday at Betahaus Sofia. Attendance is limited to 5 people per session.

Each session is facilitated to a strict structure:
1. Delegate the challenge
2. Fixers ask clarification questions
3. Fixers work with each other while others observe
4. Final presentation
5. Feedback from the challenger
